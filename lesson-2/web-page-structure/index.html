Gin Caesar

This is an exotic drink that will blow ya mind! This is a Clamato based cocktail that will blow ya mind!

Ingredients

  • 2oz Gin
  • 4 dashes of Worcestershire
  • 3 dashes of hot sauce
  • Horseradish
  • Clamato juice
  • Celery

Steps

  1. In a large glass add ice
  2. Combine gin with hot sauce and Worcestershire
  3. Pour into large glass over the ice
  4. Drink it!
